The Challenge

Translate the world's most complex pharmaceutical data into visuals that make a boardroom stop, look, and decide.

The Context

IQVIA is the world's largest provider of pharma data analytics. Their clients — global drug companies, health systems, regulators — make billion-dollar decisions from data. Design has to earn its place at that table.

The Problem

Dense datasets, multi-market comparisons, clinical trial timelines, and market access reports arrived as raw numbers. The ask: make these compelling, scannable, and persuasive without sacrificing accuracy or compliance.

The Constraint

Strict brand compliance, tight turnaround (48–72hr standard), zero tolerance for data error, and multi-format deliverables — PPT, PDF, digital, and print — all required from a single source file system.

Design Methodology

Structure first.
Story second.
Colour last.

Every infographic at IQVIA followed a rigorous methodology — not a template. The discipline came from understanding that pharmaceutical audiences are expert readers who lose trust the moment design overshadows data.

01
Data Audit
Interrogate the dataset. Identify the single most important number or trend. Everything else is support.
02
Hierarchy Mapping
Map data into visual hierarchy: headline stat → supporting metrics → context → source. Never violate reading order.
03
Chart Selection
Match the chart type to the data story — never decoration. Bar for comparison, line for trend, scatter for correlation, proportional area for scale.
04
Brand Compliance
IQVIA blue/teal system applied with strict color budget: primary, secondary, accent only. No decorative color. Visual clarity over brand expression.
05
Multi-format Output
Single source file exports to PPT slide, A4 PDF, digital social cut-down, and web asset — with zero manual rework.

The Production Process

01
Brief Intake
Receive raw data, client brief, and format requirements. Define the single key insight that drives the layout.
02
Wireframe
Sketch the information architecture. Establish hierarchy, grid, and white space before any design decisions.
03
Design
Build in Illustrator or InDesign using the live component library. Charts built as editable vectors — no screenshots.
04
QA Review
Data accuracy check against source, brand compliance audit, accessibility check (contrast ratios), and copy proofread.
05
Multi-format Export
Automated export via InDesign scripting to all required formats. 48–72hr standard turnaround maintained across 4 years.
