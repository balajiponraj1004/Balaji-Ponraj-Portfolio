Overview

Two Locations.
One Bold Brand.

Cake Dudes is a fast-casual food chain selling burgers, pizzas, and cakes — operating across Canada and Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. The brand needed to feel bold enough to stop scrollers on social media, polished enough for print and packaging, and flexible enough to work across two culturally distinct markets.

The challenge: build an identity that was fun without being childish, premium without being pretentious, and recognizable across every touchpoint from a social post to a delivery box.

The Problem

No system.
No consistency.

The business had a name and a product, but no cohesive visual identity. Different materials used different fonts, colours, and styles — making it impossible to build brand equity or stand out in a competitive food market.

Objectives

Build bold.
Build consistent.

Create a complete, ownable brand identity system that could be applied consistently across print, digital, packaging, merchandise, and motion — in two countries, by multiple team members, without losing visual integrity.

Visual Identity System

Bold. Playful. Ownable.

The identity is built on a high-contrast, high-energy palette. Red and yellow signal energy and appetite — colours proven to drive hunger and brand recall in the food service industry. Black grounds the system with premiumness. Every element is designed to work at any scale, from a 2×2 sticker to a full vehicle wrap.

Creative Process

From blank canvas
to brand system.

01
Brand Audit
Analysed the competitive F&B landscape in both Canada and Thanjavur. Identified white space for a bold, high-energy identity that would stand out across both markets.
02
Logo Design
Explored 15+ logo concepts. Refined to three production variants — Primary (Red), Secondary (Yellow), and Round Badge — each optimised for specific applications.
03
Pattern Development
Created bespoke background patterns (EPS + AI) that scale seamlessly from packaging to signage — adding texture without clutter.
04
Collateral Production
Designed and produced 12+ touchpoints: menu cards, business cards, stickers, merchandise, standees, social media templates, and a vehicle wrap.
05
Motion & Digital
Produced Instagram video templates in After Effects, AI-guided digital ad creatives, and a full Facebook banner system for ongoing campaign use.
06
Handoff & Guidelines
Delivered a comprehensive brand document (InDesign + PDF), all source files (AI, EPS, CDR), font licensing guidance, and a print-ready sticker sheet.
Multi-Market Adaptation

One brand.
Two markets.

The Cake Dudes brand was designed with intentional flexibility — the core identity (Dude Red + Party Yellow + Midnight Black) is market-agnostic, while location-specific assets (Thanjavur menu pricing, local social media posts, regional promotions) were customised without breaking the visual system.

🇨🇦
Canada Location
English-primary assets, CAD pricing, social media posts in English and Tamil, seasonal promotions (Christmas, Diwali), digital-first campaign materials.
🇮🇳
Thanjavur (TNJ) Location
Tamil-script adapted assets, INR pricing, local festival promotions (Pongal, Deepavali), standees and signage in regional format sizes, QR-coded menus.
