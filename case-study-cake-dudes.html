Bold Brand Voice
The Challenge

Build a brand loud enough to own the street — without losing the warmth that keeps people coming back.

Problem

A fast-growing F&B chain needed a brand identity that could punch above its weight — bold enough to compete on street-level visibility, smart enough to work across every touchpoint.

Insight

The "Dudes" name signals personality and irreverence. The brand needed to lean into that energy — confident, playful, and unapologetically bold — with red and yellow as its battle colours.

Solution

A high-contrast red-and-yellow system built for maximum visual impact: bold mark, tight typography, and an adaptable design language that scales from sticker to signage to social reel.

The Brief

From logo to
everywhere

The brief was total: build an identity system that could handle a menu rollout, a merchandise line, exterior signage, social media campaigns, AI-generated advertising, and motion graphics — all cohesively and at speed.

The system had to be modular enough for a small team to execute day-to-day without losing brand integrity. Every template, every colour rule, every typography decision was made with that scalability in mind.

The brand's typographic voice is deliberately loud — heavy weight, tight tracking, maximum impact. Display headlines use Fraunces Black (900) for authority; body copy uses Plus Jakarta Sans for legibility at any size.
