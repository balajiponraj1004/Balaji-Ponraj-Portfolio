Overview

The Brief

Manam Canteen needed more than a logo — they needed a brand identity with the warmth and authenticity of home cooking, combined with the visual confidence to compete in Canada's crowded food service market.

The challenge was balancing cultural richness with modern appeal: creating something that felt deeply Indian yet immediately accessible to a diverse Canadian audience — without resorting to clichés.

The Problem

No visual identity.
No brand story.

The restaurant was operating without any consistent visual language. Menus, social media, and signage were mismatched, making it impossible to build brand recognition or communicate the quality of the food and experience.

Objective

Build a brand
from the ground up.

Design a complete, scalable brand identity system that communicates warmth, authenticity, and quality. The system needed to work across print collateral, digital platforms, signage, packaging, and video — with room to grow as the business expanded.

Creative Process

How we got there.

01
Brand Discovery
Deep-dive into the restaurant's story, target audience, competitive landscape in the Canadian food market, and the cultural values to be communicated.
02
Moodboarding
Created directional moodboards blending South Indian visual culture, modern editorial design, and premium restaurant aesthetics. Signed off by stakeholders.
03
Logo Development
Explored 12 logo concepts across three visual directions — wordmark, emblem, and combination mark. Refined to primary, secondary, and round logo variations.
04
System Build
Developed the complete colour system, typography hierarchy, brand patterns, illustration style, and photography direction as an integrated design system.
05
Collateral Production
Applied the brand system across 10+ touchpoints: menus, social media templates, packaging, signage, business proposal, and promotional video.
06
Brand Guidelines
Delivered comprehensive brand guidelines, editable source files, and a franchise manual to ensure consistent application as the brand grows.
